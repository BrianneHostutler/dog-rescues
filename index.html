Dog Adoption in New Jersey

Looking for a new furry friend? Why not adopt one from an animal shelter or rescue? New Jersey has many dog rescues and shelters located throughout the state. Below is a listing of NJ dog rescues and shelters, organized by county. Find one nearby and go meet your new best friend!

If you know of a dog rescue that should be added to the list, please email us at dogrescuesofnj@gmail.com.
